Arcs
-----
Feature segmentation:

Change arc to a line with desired segmentation
Segmentation can be done with a defined angle or a length of line

Angle direction:

ClockWise
Counter ClockWise

For polygon:
Arc can be converted to polygon by Pie Segment or Chord

.. image:: images/en/SettingsArc.png
